About

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in the Department of Informatics at New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT), specializing in the application of artificial intelligence and machine learning to medical image analysis and captioning. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering from Islamic University of Technology.

My research focuses on developing innovative solutions for automated medical diagnostics, specifically through the creation of a medical image captioning framework.

From 2019 to 2022, I was a Lecturer and Senior Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Uttara University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Additionally, from 2020 to 2022, I held a position of Adjunct Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Islamic University of Technology (IUT), Bangladesh.

My current research interests include Health Informatics, Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Image Processing, and Natural Language Processing (NLP).
